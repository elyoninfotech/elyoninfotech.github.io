Impactful ERP Solutions



Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) systems enable business organizations to integrate core functions such as product processes, supply lines, quality control, inventory and warehousing along with sales, accounting, human resources management and service delivery. The suite of applications allows enterprises to have a unified access to all data that can streamline their operations across various units in order to improve overall communication, increase efficiency, thereby reducing operational costs and boosting productivity.

Our Approach

A sound ERP software solution needs to work in tandem with existing processes and systems in the enterprise. The ERP suite of solutions from Elyon Infotech encompasses all the aspects of business in order to provide a synergy between resources – human, material, finance and equipment. Our offerings are applicable to a gamut of industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, finance, communications etc. We offer intelligent software to let your business processes collaborate smoothly, for quick and rapid decision making to facilitate an increased productivity and business growth.

We offer boutique and tailor-made solutions across a wide range of ERP strategies and software solutions, with our expertise to provide you with state-of- the-art and easy to use tools. All stages of development, implementation, testing, deployment, transitioning, training, maintenance, and upgrades are undertaken by us with meticulous planning and impeccable execution, by our experienced technical and domain experts.

Business Intelligence

Our smart BI tools allow your business users to share and analyze data from across the enterprise via a centralized ERP database. We provide you with dashboards, business reports and analysis tools that can empower you with informed decision making abilities.

Procurement Automation

Our ERP purchasing software will streamline your vendor management, automate purchase orders, and track payments. Our solutions will smooth your purchase order workflow, including all stakeholders and approval heads.

Financial Management

Our solutions offer modules to manage your finance flows, billing, payment accounts, general ledgers and fixed assets. We deliver you the capability to collate all your sales and operational costs in a centralized accounting system.

Human Resources Management

Our centralized HR system allows you to keep track of your employees work hours, manage staff, and aid in effective employee performance appraisals. From recruitment to time-tracking, our tools will automate your workforce management, payroll, compensation, benefits and perks.

Manufacturing and Distribution

Our offerings for ERP manufacturing will help you to manage effectively your product configuration, job costs and bills of materials. A real-time view of your business’s supply and demand levels will help you control your stocks and understand trends. Our modules include material requirement planning, capacity planning, work-order management, forecasting, scheduling and shop-floor tracking.
