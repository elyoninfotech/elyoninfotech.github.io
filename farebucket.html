Our Approach

Travelling and holidaying is an important form of leisure. Exploring new places and getting a first-hand experience of new locales is the best way to know the various cultures of the world. Holidays are proven remedies to unwind and rejuvenate, providing a much needed break from the hectic paced modern lives.

Planning is indeed the most strenuous part of the holiday. Arrangements need to be made well in advance for transport, accommodation, detailed itinerary, food, entertainment and local sightseeing. The web has made online advance bookings a certain possibility, yet coordinating all the aspects of the holiday can be a daunting task.

Helping to saving time, hassle and costs on travel is the Farebucket travel solution by Elyon Infotech. We have brought forth a unified online travel portal, which offers end-to- end services for planning all aspects of a holiday. From flight, train, car and bus bookings, hotel bookings, arrangement for food, entertainment, local attractions and schedule planning, our portal is a single-stop shop for travel planning. With focus on quality travel and use of state-of- the-art technology to get the best deals for their customers, our Farebucket offering is an innovative and intuitive solution to create perfect holidays for you.
