Web Development



A website is a digital extension to a business in today’s world and embrace global sensibility. A website is often the gateway to communicate freely with your partners, customers and visitors. The website allows business transactions to be conducted round the clock, transcending geographies and time-zones. This lets you expand your footprint and empowers you to market your products and services more effectively.

Our Approach

An enterprise’s website is the virtual face of the company. The presentation of data and information on the website and the design of the look and feel is an aesthetic exercise, whereas the supporting functionality requires technological innovations. The web development solutions from Elyon Infotech include both the aspects of website design.

User-friendly website

We create websites that keep your customer in central focus. Our web solutions target the usability aspect as much as the nature of business. Effectively mapping of the end-users and the company’s products/services, our experts deliver fresh and innovative websites that are attractive as well as easy to navigate. The websites are quick to load to ensure higher engagement levels.

Tools for enhanced persuasive selling

Our boutique solutions are a package of well-placed objects, schemes, fonts, colors, imagery and content that can help you tell a compelling story. Our web solutions are developed with methods that promote your company’s professionalism, expertise and abilities, thus aiding in business growth.

End-to- end strategy

From web-designing, coding, markup writing, testing, deployment and training to upgrade and maintenance support, our services encompass all phases of web development.

Integration support

Our expert solutions enable seamless integration of your website to internal processes at the backend - CRM, ERP, HR, billing, payment systems, data integration, security, interfacing with partners’ digital systems. We support all forms of interoperability solutions with the website.

Wrappers and from-scratch variants

Depending upon the preferences and budgets, we offer websites developed from scratch or using existing tools with wrappers on top to customize the look as per the specific requirement. Our transparent execution enables our customers to know the tools being adopted.

Agile mode

Well versed with agile methodologies as Daikibo, Kanban and Scrum, our solutions follow an iterative approach, so there can be lighter and quicker versions ready, to reduce costs, increase flexibility and accommodate changes to requirements.
